## Overview

IT For Prof provides comprehensive IT infrastructure administration, including outsourcing, maintenance, support, and network configuration. Follow these steps to create your account, book a consultation, sign agreements, and access your client dashboard. You'll be up and running in under 15 minutes.

<Callout kind="info">

**Prerequisites**

- A business email address (e.g., admin@yourcompany.com)
- Company details: name, address, and contact phone
- Access to a modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)

No credit card required for initial signup.

</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Begin by signing up for a free account. This process takes about 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Signup Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://app.itforprof.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your business name, email, and phone number. Create a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from `noreply@itforprof.com`. Click the link to confirm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  If you don't receive the email, check spam or request a resend from the signup page.
</Callout>

## Book Your Initial Consultation

Schedule a free 30-minute consultation with an IT specialist to discuss your infrastructure needs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Booking" icon="globe">
    Log in to `https://app.itforprof.com` and select **Book Consultation** from the dashboard.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Choose Service" icon="calendar">
        Select **IT Outsourcing Assessment**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Pick Time" icon="clock">
        View available slots and book one that fits your schedule.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Confirm" icon="check-circle">
        Receive confirmation email with Zoom link.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Phone Booking" icon="phone">
    Call our support line at `+1-800-IT-PROF` (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EST).

    Provide your account email and preferred consultation time. An agent will confirm availability.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Sign Service Agreements

After consultation, review and sign digital agreements securely.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review Proposal" icon="file-text">
    Check your email for the customized proposal from your consultant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Electronically" icon="edit-3">
    Log in to the dashboard, navigate to **Agreements**, and use DocuSign to sign.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate Services" icon="zap">
    Once signed, services activate within 24 hours. You'll get a welcome email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the Client Dashboard

Monitor tickets, view reports, and manage services from your centralized dashboard.

1. Go to `https://app.itforprof.com/login`
2. Enter your email and password
3. Use two-factor authentication if enabled

Key sections:
- **Tickets**: Track support requests
- **Reports**: View uptime and performance metrics
- **Billing**: Manage invoices
- **Team**: Add users

<Expandable title="Troubleshooting Login Issues" default-open="false">
  - Forgot password? Use the reset link on the login page.
  - Locked out? Contact support@itforprof.com.
  - Browser issues? Clear cache or try incognito mode.
</Expandable>

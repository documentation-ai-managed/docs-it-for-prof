---
title: How to Schedule a Maintenance Request
description: Send a maintenance request to IT For Prof.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the client portal">
    Visit https://portal.itforprof.com and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to 'Requests'">
    Choose 'Maintenance Request' from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the request form">
    Include issue details, preferred date/time, and urgency.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and wait for confirmation">
    You will receive an email once the request is scheduled.
  </Step>
</Steps>

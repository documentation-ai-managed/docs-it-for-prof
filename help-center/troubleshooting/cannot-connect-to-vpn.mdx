## Symptoms

- Unable to access company resources remotely
- VPN client shows connection error

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your internet connection">
    ````bash
    ping google.com
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Restart VPN client">
    Close and relaunch your VPN application.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact IT For Prof support">
    Email support@itforprof.com if issues persist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
